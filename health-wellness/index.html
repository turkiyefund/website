HEALTH & WELLNESS

EVOO is so much more than a superfood and the benefits extend well beyond the kitchen. As one of the world’s healthiest fats, it’s a powerful tool for bolstering your overall health and wellness and, when combined with a balanced diet and healthy lifestyle, can benefit your heart, promote weight management, kick up your skincare routine and so much more. Time to Eat and Live Well!

HEART HEALTH

Studies suggest a diet high in EVOO can significantly decrease the risk of cardiovascular disease, inhibit “bad”? cholesterol, lower blood pressure, and prevent strokes – even more reason to dip and drizzle to your heart’s content!

INFLAMMATION

Beyond its versatility in the kitchen, EVOO is one of the most important ingredients in an anti-inflammatory diet.

Rich in antioxidants called polyphenols, cold-pressed EVOO contains the compound oleocanthal, which has a similar anti-inflammatory response to ibuprofen in the body.

BRAIN HEALTH

Studies have found that a diet rich in EVOO can boost brain power, improve memory and positively impact your mental health.

It may even help reduce your risk of developing Alzheimer’s disease.

GUT HEALTH

Did you know there’s a connection between a healthy gut and overall well-being, including a robust immune system?

EVOO is rich in nutrients and antibacterial properties that can help eliminate harmful bacteria in the gut. In fact, studies suggest EVOO may kill the bacterium that can infect the stomach, leading to ulcers and even cancer.

WEIGHT MANAGEMENT

Research has shown that the Mediterranean diet can help you lose weight and keep the weight off longer, partly because it relies on EVOO as its primary source of fat.

Using EVOO instead of Vegetable or Canola oil can help you shed even more unwanted kilos.
