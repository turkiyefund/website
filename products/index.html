OUR PRODUCTS

We care deeply how our olive is produced, harvested and extracted. We aim to provide the finest quality olive oil while ensuring a positive, social and sustainable future.

From start to finish, you can taste the difference in Mago olive oils because our olive farmers and olive oil craftsmen have carefully nurtured the world’s best olives from grove to table, creating the best-tasting, highest-quality olive oil. Our olive oils are ideal for sautéing, grilling, baking, frying and roasting. Each one adds flavor, depth and balance to your everyday salads, soups, marinades and anything else you can dream up in the kitchen.

Mago’s farmer-crafted, high-quality olive oils help you Eat and Live Well every day. The only thing left to do is decide which Mago olive oil is right for you.

Robust

Robust and lively, ideal as a finishing oil or used to grill beef and lamb dishes for a full bodied, peppery taste.
